Functionality

What Sofra does

A complete meal-management system for the household — planning, tracking, and insight, all built on your family's real food memory.

Core

Logging & memory

10-second logging

Autocomplete from your history, smart time-of-day defaults, and quick-picks make logging faster than a whiteboard.

Multi-dish meals

Log a full thali — a main plus sides — or several dishes ordered at a restaurant, in one entry.

Permanent history

Every meal is kept forever, searchable and exportable. Your kitchen finally has a memory.

Dish library

Everything your family cooks, with favourites, cuisine filters, frequency and "not made in a while".

Intelligence

Planning & insights

Auto-plan the week

Generated from your own dishes — avoids recent repeats, mixes cuisines, respects your dine-out limit.

Home vs. outside

See your home-cooked ratio and how it's trending — the pattern most families never measure.

Spending insights

Outside spend, budgets, and a monthly trend, in your own currency.

Forgotten dishes

Surfaces dishes you haven't made in a while, so your back-catalogue never gets lost.

Cuisine variety

A breakdown of how varied your cooking really is, over any range.

Shareable stat cards

Turn any insight into a branded image to share with family or on social.

Family

Household & kids

Shared household

Everyone adds and sees meals in real time — one source of truth for the kitchen.

Invite by code

Bring family members in with a simple invite code.

Kids tiffin track

Plan a separate kids menu alongside family meals, generated from the kids' own dishes.

Repeat alerts

"Sandwich three days running" — Sofra nudges you to mix up the tiffin.

Everyday

Restaurants, reminders & comfort

Restaurant tracker

Visits, spend, and last-visit per place — plus the dishes you ordered, each with your star rating.

Smart reminders

Opt-in daily (tomorrow's meals), weekly recap, and monthly-insights notifications — all on-device, no server.

Light & dark

A warm terracotta-and-sage palette in both modes, following your system or your choice.

Global by design

Any cuisine, any currency, any family — culturally warm without being narrow.

Benefits, in a sentence

Less "what's for dinner?", less takeout you didn't mean to buy, less repetition your family is tired of — and a permanent, shareable memory of the food that makes your home yours.
